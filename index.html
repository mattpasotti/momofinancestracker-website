A complete personal finance toolkit — multiple accounts, real-world transaction types, and insights that actually mean something.

Multiple accounts

Bank, credit card, and investment accounts side by side — with the right balance logic for each.

Smart transactions

Budgets & goals

Set monthly budgets and savings goals. Momo tracks the rest and resets at each period boundary.

Net worth dashboard

Assets minus liabilities, plus a 12-month trend at a glance. See where you actually stand.

Trends & insights

Spending trends, savings rate, top category changes, year-over-year comparisons, and forecasts.

Search & filter

Find any transaction by category, type, date, or amount. Sort and slice however you need.

Essentials & lifestyle

Categories grouped into Essentials and Lifestyle so you instantly see what matters most.

Bill reminders

Local notifications before bills are due — 7, 3, 1, and 0 days out. Nothing leaves your phone.

Budget alerts

PDF reports

Beautiful monthly PDF with chart, account balances, totals, and the full transaction list.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Your financial data — every transaction, budget, account, category, and note — stays on your device, in a local database. We do not have access to it.

Do you sell or share my data?

No. We never sell or share your data — and we never will. To improve the app and fix crashes, we use Google Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to collect anonymous, aggregate usage information (which screens are opened, how often the app is launched) and crash reports. Firebase does not receive any of your financial data, transaction details, category names, or notes. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Is there a free version?

Yes — the core experience is free. An optional Plus subscription unlocks advanced features. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store; we use RevenueCat to verify entitlements without seeing your financial data.

What languages are supported?

English, Italian, Spanish, French, German, and Dutch. The app follows your system language automatically.

Does it work offline?

Yes — fully. There's no login, no sync, and no internet requirement. Notifications and bill reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

How do I export my data?

Open Settings → Export & Import in the app. You can export to CSV (for spreadsheets) or to a styled monthly PDF report. Both files are saved or shared from your device only.

Is there an Android version?

Yes. Momo Finances Tracker launches on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play) at the same time, with the same features.

How do I get support?

Visit our Support page or email support@momofinancestracker.com. You can also use Settings → Help & Support directly from inside the app.
